FAQ

Q: What is DiscoverSafe?

A: DiscoverSafe is a user-friendly app designed to automatically back up your Spotify Discover Weekly playlist. It ensures that you never lose your handpicked collection of new and exciting tracks, making it easy to revisit your favorite discoveries.

Q: How does DiscoverSafe work?

A: DiscoverSafe seamlessly integrates with your Spotify account and periodically backs up your Discover Weekly playlist. It runs in the background, ensuring a hassle-free experience while safeguarding your favorite songs.

Q: Is my data secure with DiscoverSafe?

A: Absolutely! DiscoverSafe takes data privacy seriously. Your Spotify account information and playlists are stored securely, and the app does not share your data with any third parties.

Q: How do I turn off DiscoverSafe?

A: Go to your Spotify account settings, find the apps tab, and then "revoke access" from DiscoverSafe. You will now no longer back up your DiscoverSafe playlist.

Q: Why isn't there any DiscoverSafe playlist in my library?

A: If you're a new Spotify user, the app might need more time to gather enough data for Discover Weekly playlists. However, once DiscoverSafe is integrated, you won't need to worry about manually creating the playlist. Additionally, please note that during its development phase, the app has limited slots for users (up to 25 users only).
